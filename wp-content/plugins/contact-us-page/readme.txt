Create your contact page in seconds with a contact form, map, social icons and your contact info.

== Description ==
With the Contact Us Page plugin will you have a professional looking contact page in seconds. It comes with a contact form, google map, contact info fields with icons and social icons. You can replace the default contact form with the shortcode of your favorite contact form. 
<br \>
<b>Get started:</b>
1. Go to the settings page and enter your information. <br \>
2. Copy the shortcode in the settings page and paste it into a page of your choice. <br \>
3. Done! It's that simple.

== Installation ==
= Installation =
Install Contact Us Page via the plugin directory or by uploading the files manually to your server.
